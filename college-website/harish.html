Dr.K. Harish Kumar

Dr.K. Harish Kumar is an Assistant Professor in the, Department of Computer Science & Informatics, Mahatma Gandhi University, Nalgonda since 4th May 2012. His Academic Qualifications are M.Tech(CSE),Ph.D(CSE).. He Worked as Academic Consultant In the Department of Computer Science& Informatics Mahatma Gandhi University, Nalgonda from 2009-2012. He has 15 years of teaching experience. He worked as a Warden for Neelagiri Boys Hostel, MGU from 2012-2013.He also worked as a Warden for Nagarjuna Boys Hostel,UCET,MGU from 2013-2014. He worked as Vice-Principal for Nagarjuna Boys Hostel, UCET,MGU from 2014-2016., He worked as Principal for University College of Engineering and Technology,MGU from 2016-2018 .He worked as Deputy director for Hostels &Messes, MGU from 2019-2022.He worked as Head ,Dept. of CS&I from 2020-2022.He Worked as Assistant controller of Examinations,Exam Banch,MGU from 2022-2024. Presently he is acting as Secretrary ,University Sports Board,MGU. He is a member for Alumni Association, MGU. And He is also part of various College level and University level Development committees.
