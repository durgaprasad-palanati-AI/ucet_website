Principal's Message

“Education is not the learning of facts, but the training of the mind to think.” – Albert Einstein

It is my pleasure to welcome you to the University College of Engineering and Technology - MGU website. It is a privilege to be the Principal of the institution and lead such a dedicated and committed team of teachers and students.

Engineering education has become an integral and inevitable part of modern society. Education becomes meaningful when it nurtures natural talent and helps students grow into value-based individuals.

The purpose of education is to develop the ability to think and reason. This is achieved through an excellent academic environment, state-of-the-art infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, and highly qualified faculty members. UCET provides a challenging yet enriching environment for learning.

The college has a strong tradition of academic excellence and overall personality development. Our academic framework ensures a strong foundation in both theoretical and industry-oriented knowledge. We emphasize Outcome Based Education (OBE) through innovative teaching methodologies.

Students are given opportunities to interact with industry experts. Through initiatives like TASK and ICT-enabled learning, we enhance soft skills, life skills, and technical skills to make students globally competitive and entrepreneurial.

UCET also provides ample opportunities in co-curricular and extracurricular activities, encouraging students to showcase their talents and grow as responsible citizens.

I wish you a fulfilling and successful academic journey at UCET. May you contribute to the pride and prestige of this esteemed institution.

I warmly welcome you all and wish you the very best in your future endeavors.

Prof. Rekha Redamalla
M.Tech., Ph.D (Italy), LMISTE
Principal
University College of Engineering and Technology
Mahatma Gandhi University, Nalgonda
