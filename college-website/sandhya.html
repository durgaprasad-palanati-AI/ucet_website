Dr.D.Sandhya Rani

Dr.D.Sandhya Rani is working as Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Informatics, Mahatma Gandhi University, Nalgonda since 4th May 2012. She has completed her B.Tech (CSE) and M.Tech (CSE) from Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad, Ph.D(CSE) from Osmania University, Hyderabad. Her Teaching experience is 17 years. She has four research publications to her credit in National and International Journals indexed in Scopus. Presented papers at National and International and Springer Conferences. She attended a three day workshop on “Teaching, Learning & Evaluation online with Moodle MOOC platform & Open Education Resources” at NUEPA, NewDelhi. She is into different academic & administrative positions as warden for Girls Hostel, UCET, MGU, and Head of the Department. She also worked as Vice-Principal and Principal for University College of Engineering and Technology, MGU. At Present she is working as Assistant Controller of Examinations, PG Section, Examination Branch, MGU. She is the Chairperson, Board of Studies for Computer Science. She is into various College Level and University Level Committees Area of Specialization: Deep Learning with Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Database Management Systems.
