Dr. Ch. Sudha Rani

Dr. Ch. Sudha Rani is an accomplished academician with over 17 years of teaching experience in the field of Computer Science and Engineering. She earned her B.Tech degree in Computer Science and Information Technology in 2003, followed by an M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering in 2008. She was awarded her Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering in 2025. Area of Specialization: Currently, she serves as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Informatics. She has also held significant administrative responsibilities, including serving as the Head of the Department CS&I, Principal of the University College of Engineering and Technology at Mahatma Gandhi University, Nalgonda. Her research interests span across Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Optimization Techniques, and Information Security. Dr. Sudha Rani has contributed to the academic community through multiple scholarly publications, with seven research papers published in both national and international journals. She has also presented her research at four national and international conferences. In addition to her research contributions, she is the author of the book titled “Advanced Machine Learning Applications using Python Programming”, published in 2022.
