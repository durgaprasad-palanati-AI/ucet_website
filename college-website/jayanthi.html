Dr. M.JAYANTHI

Dr. M.JAYANTHI is an Assistant Professor in the, Department of Computer Science & Informatics, Mahatma Gandhi University, Nalgonda since 4th May 2012. She did her M.Tech (CSE) from JNTU Hyderabad, PhD(CSE) from Osmania University Hyderabad and AP-SET Qualified . She has 20 years of teaching experience. She worked as Warden for Girls Hostel, Vice-Principal and Principal for University College of Engineering and Technology and discharged her duties as Chairperson for AICTE approval process Committee. She worked as Incharge Placement Officer for Engineering College. She worked as a Head of the Department of Computer Science and Informatics. She Organized 1 National seminar as convenor and published 4 research papers in International journals indexing in Scopus. Presented two papers in IEEE conference and Springer Conference. Presently working as Co-ordinator for Dean CDC MGU. She took responsibility and served in various College Level and University Level Committees. Area of Specialization: Cloud Computing, Machine Learning, Big Data, Internet of Things, Computer Architecture, Distributed Systems, Operating Systems.
